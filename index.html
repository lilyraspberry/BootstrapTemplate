Welcome to our website

Discovery Bee is delighted to set up exceptional outdoor activities for you. So that all you have to do is relax and enjoy diving into the wild in complete peace.

Discovery Bee is there to provide you with memorable adventures whether you wish to:

  • go hiking for a week-end
  • hiking with a donkey
  • Horseback riding
  • enjoy stunning landscapes
  • escape from your busy city lifestyle
  • take awesome pictures of wild animals
  • go solo or with a group

We also organize special events such as stargazing for the Night of Stars

Your dream trip does not figure on our website ? Do not hesitate to tell us about it and we will do our best to set it up for you.
